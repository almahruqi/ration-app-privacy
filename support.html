How to get help

For questions about the app, feature requests, or to report an issue, email us at the address above. We aim to respond within a few business days.

Privacy & data

Your data stays on your device. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers. For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

App information

Ration is a grocery and home shopping budget tracker. Create lists, track purchases, and manage your weekly or monthly budget. The app works offline and supports English and Arabic.
